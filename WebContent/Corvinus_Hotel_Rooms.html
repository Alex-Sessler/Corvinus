All our rooms are comfortable, newly renovated and equipped with en suite bathrooms with bathtub or shower, WC, hairdryer, satellite TV, radio, international direct dial telephone, in-room safe, WLAN Internet connection, minibar and with air-condition.

Our rooms are Non-smoking.

Our facilities include room service, coffee and tea making facilities, express laundry, iron and ironing board available, shoeshine service, baby cots, luggage storage, elevator, reservations for sightseeing tours, theatre and concerts.

Airport transfers to and from Vienna International Airport available at a charge.
