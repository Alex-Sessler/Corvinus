Location

Located in the heart of the Austrian capital, the Hotel Pension Corvinus Vienna is justa short walk from all the city's major attractions, shops and restaurants.

For visitors interested in art and culture, our hotel is the perfect place to stay as it is close to major museums, galleries and theatres. The hotel will be happy to arrange tickets for their guests for the opera, theatre and ballet. If you prefer something less formal, we recommend the numerous hour-long concerts held in the various churches and palaces in Vienna.

By car
The Hotel Pension Corvinus is easily accessed from three major motorways,the A1 from the west, the A2 from the south and the A4 from the east of Austria.


By air
Our hotel is 30 minutes away by taxi from Vienna International Airport and less than 45 minutes by public transport.
The reception team is always happy to help guests with airline bookings.

By public transportation
Underground line U 3 directly in front of the hotel (station: "Neubaugasse")also the bus line 13 A (station: "Kirchengasse")
