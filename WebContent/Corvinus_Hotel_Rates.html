EARLY CHECK IN / LATE CHECK OUT
If your arrival is early in the day and you prefer immediate access to your room, we recommend reserving for the prior night to guarantee immediate access. Similarly, for late departures, reserving an additional night will guarantee access until you leave the Hotel. If you choose not to reserve, we will be glad to store your luggage.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Cancellations for a hotel room reservation must be received 72 hours prior to the expected day of arrival, and may differ by arrival date and room type.
If cancellation of a guaranteed reservation is not received by the required date, the Hotel will charge for one night's accommodation.
In case of cancellation on arrival day or No-Show - 100% of the booking amount  will be charged.
For details of cancellation policies and deposit requirements,  please check at time of booking.  

CHILDREN POLICY
Children from 0-3 years can share the room with their parents at no extra charge. Children from 4-12 years, receive 50 % discount in the room of their parents. Children 13 years and older, third person rates will apply and an extra bed  will be charged.
Baby cot - no extra charge.
