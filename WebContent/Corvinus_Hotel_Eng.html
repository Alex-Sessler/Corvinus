Welcome to the Corvinus Hotel's website.

Our hotel is named after Matthias Corvinus (1443-1490)  King of Hungary from 1458 to his death. He also claimed the King of Bohemia and Austria.

The 4 star Corvinus Hotel Pension is situated in the lively, trendy area of the famous Mariahilfer Strasse, which is the main shopping street of the City, with the city centre just minutes away. The nearby “Neubaugasse” metro station on the U3 lines provides convenient, efficient access from and to all areas of the region.
The famous "Ring Strasse" and the historical sites like the State Opera,

The down town and St Stephan´s Cathedral and the Imperial Palace are in walking distance.

Public transport - the underground railway station “Neubaugasse” on the line U 3 just in front of the hotel.

Lying just 30 minutes by car from Vienna´s International Airport.
The hotel offers parking garage and airport transfers for guests.
We wish you a pleasant stay with us in Vienna !
